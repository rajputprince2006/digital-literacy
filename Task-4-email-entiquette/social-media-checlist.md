 Do’s 

Use privacy settings to control who can see your posts and personal information.

Think before posting to ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.

Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.

Maintain a positive and professional online presence.

Use social media for learning, networking, and staying informed.




 Don’ts

Do not share sensitive personal information like address, phone number, or passwords.

Avoid posting offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content.

Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.

Avoid spending excessive time on social media that affects your studies.

Do not trust or click on suspicious links or unknown sources.
